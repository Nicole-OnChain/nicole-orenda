About

The Story Behind the Name

N·O
Nicole Orenda
Author & Creative Producer

Nicole Orenda writes fantasy worlds where elemental magic meets impossible choices, where romance is earned through danger, and where every chapter leaves you needing the next.

Under Nicole Orenda LLC, she builds serialized fiction, ebooks, and digital education content designed for readers who want more than a book -- they want a world to inhabit.

Her debut series, Breath of Earth, launches in 2026. The franchise spans three Parts across twelve seasons -- a sweeping fantasy romance about Wind, Earth, and the people who dare to break the system that divides them.
